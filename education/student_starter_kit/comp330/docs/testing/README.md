# Testing Package

## Purpose

This directory helps teams verify engineering claims.

Testing is not simply finding bugs.

Testing creates evidence.

Testing helps teams understand whether engineering behavior matches expectations.

Keep testing practical.

Do not over-engineer it.

---

## Testing Artifacts

| Artifact | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| `test-strategy.md` | Defines the overall testing approach |
| `test-plan.md` | Defines what will be tested and when |
| `test-cases.md` | Defines specific validation activities |
| `ci-evidence.md` | Records automated validation evidence |
| `test-evidence/` | Stores supporting testing evidence |

---

## Relationship To ETIS Philosophy

Testing answers one question:

> How do we know our claims are true?

Students should continuously ask:

- What are we verifying?
- How are we verifying it?
- What evidence exists?
- What remains unverified?

---

## Testing Progression

This directory intentionally supports the following progression.

```text
Testing Strategy

↓

Testing Plan

↓

Testing Cases

↓

Automated Validation

↓

Testing Evidence
```

Strong engineering teams continuously verify assumptions.

---

## Review Questions

Teams should periodically ask:

- Are requirements being tested?
- Is evidence accumulating?
- Are gaps visible?
- Are risks being discovered early?

---

## Engineering Standard

Testing creates evidence that engineering claims are true.
