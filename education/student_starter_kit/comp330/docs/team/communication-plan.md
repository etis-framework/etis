## Escalation Process

If blockers occur:

1. Discuss within the team.
2. Escalate to the appropriate role owner.
3. Escalate to the instructor if necessary.

Do not allow blockers to remain hidden.

---

## Communication Expectations

Team members should:

- communicate early
- communicate respectfully
- communicate consistently

---

## Review Questions

- Are important decisions visible?
- Are blockers being surfaced quickly?
- Is communication becoming a bottleneck?

---

## Engineering Standard

Strong engineering teams make important information visible before it becomes a problem.
