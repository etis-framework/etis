# Working Agreements

## Purpose

This document defines how the team will operate together.

Working agreements should be practical, realistic, and adjustable.

---

## Team Agreements

### Communication

We will:

- respond within agreed timeframes
- communicate blockers early
- ask questions when uncertain

### Reviews

We will:

- review engineering work continuously
- provide constructive feedback
- challenge ideas, not people

### AI Usage

We will:

- disclose meaningful AI usage
- verify AI outputs
- maintain human ownership

### Meetings

We will:

- arrive prepared
- respect each other's time
- document important decisions

### Accountability

We will:

- complete assigned work
- ask for help when needed
- surface risks early

---

## Revisiting Agreements

Review agreements periodically.

Adjust if they are no longer helping the team succeed.

---

## Engineering Standard

Healthy engineering teams intentionally define how they work together.
