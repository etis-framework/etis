# Assumptions and Open Questions

## Purpose

This document preserves assumptions, uncertainties, unresolved questions, and decisions that require future clarification.

Assumptions and open questions are engineering evidence.

They make uncertainty visible rather than hiding it inside team discussions, AI outputs, or undocumented decisions.

## Review Questions

Review this document regularly and ask:

* Which assumptions are still valid?
* Which assumptions have become risks?
* Which open questions block requirements, design, testing, or release readiness?
* Which questions need stakeholder input?
* Which questions were answered by AI, and how were those answers verified?

## Engineering Standard

Uncertainty is not a failure.

Hidden uncertainty is an engineering risk.

Assumptions and open questions should remain visible until resolved, accepted, or converted into risks.
