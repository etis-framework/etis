# Quality Package

## Purpose

This directory helps teams track and learn from defects.

Quality is not perfection.

Quality is the disciplined practice of finding problems, understanding them, fixing them, and learning from them.

Keep quality evidence lightweight.

Do not over-engineer it.

---

## Quality Artifacts

| Artifact | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| `defect-log.md` | Records known defects and their status |
| `defect-review.md` | Reviews defect patterns and improvement actions |

---

## Relationship To ETIS Philosophy

Everything important leaves evidence.

Defects are engineering evidence.

Teams should ask:

- What failed?
- Why did it fail?
- What evidence exists?
- What was fixed?
- What did we learn?

---

## Engineering Standard

Strong teams do not hide defects.

Strong teams make defects visible and learn from them.
