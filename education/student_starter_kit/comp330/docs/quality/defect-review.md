# Defect Review

## Purpose

This document helps the team review defect patterns and improve engineering practice.

The goal is learning.

Defect review should help the team understand what is repeatedly going wrong and what needs to improve.

## Pattern Review

Look for patterns such as:

- unclear requirements
- weak validation
- missing tests
- poor error handling
- misunderstood AI output
- integration problems
- operational visibility gaps

## Review Questions

- Are similar defects appearing repeatedly?
- Are defects caused by unclear requirements?
- Are tests missing?
- Did AI introduce or hide any defect?
- What should the team change?

---

## Engineering Standard

Defects should improve the engineering system, not merely the code.
