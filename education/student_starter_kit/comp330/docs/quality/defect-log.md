# Defect Log

## Purpose

This document records known defects, issues, failures, and quality problems.

The goal is visibility.

Do not hide defects.

Defects are engineering evidence.

## Status Values

Use these status values:

- Open
- In Progress
- Fixed
- Verified
- Deferred
- Accepted Risk

---

## Review Questions

- Are important defects visible?
- Are severe defects connected to risks?
- Are fixed defects verified?
- Are deferred defects documented honestly?

---

## Engineering Standard

A defect is not resolved because it was discussed.

A defect is resolved when the fix is implemented, verified, and documented.
