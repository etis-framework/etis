The goal is not to duplicate the full traceability record.

The goal is to help reviewers quickly understand whether the release is defensible.

## Review Questions

- Does every release claim have evidence?
- Are unsupported claims removed or marked as limitations?
- Are risks connected to release decisions?
- Can another engineer follow the evidence?

---

## Engineering Standard

A release claim is not defensible unless it can be traced to evidence.
