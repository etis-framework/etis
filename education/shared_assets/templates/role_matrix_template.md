# Role Matrix Template

**Classification:** ETIS Educational Ecosystem Shared Asset  
**Category:** Template  
**Origin:** Loyola University Chicago COMP 330 — Software Engineering  
**Authoritative Format:** Markdown  
**Typical Repository Location:** `/docs/team/role-matrix.md`

## Purpose

This template assigns operational ownership and backups for team roles, repository evidence, review responsibilities, and release readiness.

Roles define accountability. They do not limit contribution. Every student is a developer and engineering contributor first.

---

## Engineering Guidance

Every student is a developer and engineering contributor first.

Operational roles define ownership, not permission. Any team member may update evidence artifacts, but one owner is responsible for keeping each artifact correct and ready at the proper point in the cycle.

Teams of 3-5 may combine roles. Every role should still have a primary owner and backup.

Role ownership should make engineering evidence visible, reviewable, and resilient when a team member is unavailable.

## Bottom Line

A role matrix makes ownership visible. It helps the team avoid hidden work, single-person dependency, and deadline surprises. It should support accountability without turning roles into walls.
