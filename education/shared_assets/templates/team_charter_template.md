## Purpose

This template defines the team operating agreement: purpose, communication, cadence, decision rules, accountability expectations, and escalation path.

A team charter is not a legal contract. It is a lightweight engineering agreement that helps the team coordinate work, preserve expectations, and avoid silent drift.

---

## Engineering Guidance

Keep this lightweight and useful.

Review it during project launch and update it if the team changes its workflow.

Every team member is a developer and engineering contributor, regardless of role.

The charter should help the team make expectations visible before serious implementation begins.

---

## 4. Professional Accountability Expectations

Every team member must contribute visible work in GitHub during the semester.

Every meaningful change should connect to an issue, branch, pull request, review, test/evidence update, or decision record.

AI-assisted work must be disclosed, reviewed, verified, and explainable by the team.

Missed meetings, missed commitments, or invisible work should be surfaced early, not hidden until a deadline.

Peer reviews should be evidence-based and professional.
