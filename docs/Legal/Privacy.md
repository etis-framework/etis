# Privacy

_Last updated: June 2026_

This site is intended to be a static publication site for *Engineering Trustworthy Intelligent Systems*.

The site itself does not intentionally collect personal information through accounts, forms, comments, mailing lists, payment systems, or site-level tracking scripts.

## ETIS Privacy Philosophy

ETIS is built around engineering trust.

The website intentionally minimizes data collection and unnecessary tracking.

The objective is to provide a professional publication experience without creating unnecessary digital footprints for readers.

Personal information should be collected only when there is a clear and legitimate reason to do so.

## Hosting

This site is expected to be hosted through GitHub Pages or similar static-site infrastructure. The hosting provider may collect standard server, access, diagnostic, or security logs according to its own policies.

## Analytics and Tracking

Current status: no intentional site-level analytics, advertising trackers, behavioral tracking, or user-profiling tools are configured by this publication site.

If analytics, contact forms, mailing lists, embedded services, or other third-party tools are added later, this page should be updated to describe those services clearly.

## External Links

This site may link to external resources, repositories, publications, standards, tools, or organizations. External sites are governed by their own privacy practices.

## Contact

Questions about this site or its privacy statement may be directed through the contact information provided on the Contact page.
