# Looking Ahead to Volume II

Engineering does not end at release.

Once systems enter production environments, organizations assume a new set of responsibilities.

These responsibilities include:

- Operating systems continuously
- Monitoring behavior
- Responding to incidents
- Governing AI participation over time
- Reducing complexity
- Preserving organizational knowledge
- Sustaining trust over years rather than releases

Volume II addresses these operational responsibilities.

The focus shifts from building systems to stewarding systems.

Readers will discover that trustworthy intelligent systems are not defined by successful deployments alone. They are defined by an organization's ability to sustain trust over time.
