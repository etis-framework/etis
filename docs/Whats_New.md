# What's New

This page records major public milestones for the ETIS ecosystem.

Updates are intentionally curated.

ETIS is a stewarded engineering framework rather than a continuously changing software product. Significant additions, publications, educational products, and ecosystem enhancements are recorded here.

---

## June 2026

### ETIS First Edition Public Release (Version 1.0)

The first public release of the ETIS ecosystem is available.

This release establishes ETIS as a complete public framework for engineering trustworthy intelligent systems in the AI era.

### Book Publication

The ETIS First Edition is complete and publicly available.

Publication formats now include:

- Complete online edition
- Downloadable PDF edition
- Two-volume edition
- Amazon KDP paperback editions
- Amazon KDP hardcover editions

The Amazon editions are published as a professionally organized two-volume series designed for long-term reference, teaching, and professional use.

---

### Two-Volume Edition

The book is also available as a professionally organized two-volume edition.

**Volume I**

Foundations and Engineering Construction

**Volume II**

Operations, Governance, Oversight, and Stewardship

The two-volume edition improves navigation while preserving the integrity of the complete framework.
